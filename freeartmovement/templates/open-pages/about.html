    What is Free Art Friday?

    Free Art Fridays is a global event where artist randomly gift their artwork to their comminity by randomly leaving it on the streets. Some make a game out of it and leave clues on social media for people to find.

    Creating a platform.

    Now, we are going to take Free Art Friday to the next level by tossing a little technology in to the mix. The rules are still the same only now, it's an all out scavenger hunt!
